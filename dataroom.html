Architecture
Thin-film lithium niobate (TFLN) platform at 1550 nm telecom wavelength, CMOS-compatible fabrication
Loop architecture — recirculating time-bin encoding replaces large spatial MZM arrays, dramatically reducing footprint
On-chip optical non-linear activation eliminates the optical-to-electrical conversion bottleneck
Inverse power scaling: efficiency improves as N grows — opposite to GPU O(N²) cost

Matrix fidelity 98.5% · Inference accuracy 99%
